The R-99 is rightly feared and coveted by a majority of Apex Legends players, rightly so as the fastest-firing gun in the game. Our Apex Legends R-99 guide will walk you through various useful things to know about this supremely potent SMG, from damage and fire rate to time-to-kill (TTK) stats. So let’s get started, and see why the R-99 is the favourite SMG of many an Apex Legends veteran.

R-301

¨ The Apex Legends R-301 is a Assault Rifle that uses Light Ammo and does 336 Damage Per Second with a maximum of 14 Damage Per Shot. It takes 3.2s to reload an empty magazine and 2.4s if you have some ammo already in the gun. The R-301 can attach Barrel Stabilizer, Extended Light Mag, Standard Stock, 1x Holo, 1x-2x Variable Holo, 1x HCOG Classic, 2x HCOG Bruiser, 3x HCOG Ranger, 2x-4x Variable AOG.
